Chicken Wrap

11 votes

Ingredients

  • Cooked Chicken
  • Romaine Lettuce
  • Roma Tomatoes
  • Red Onion
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Chick Fil A Sauce or BBQ Sauce
  • Flour Tortillas
  • Please note that you need at least 9 inch sized tortillas to make this wrap work. These are the Burrito Sized tortillas in the store.

Steps

  1. Shredded chicken is very easy to make for this recipe or any other recipe. For this recipe you will need just under a pound of chicken. Cover the chicken breast completely with water and bring to a boil. Simmer for 4 to 10 minutes. The thickness of the breasts will determine how long you need to cook it. Cook until it reaches an internal temperature of 165 degrees.
  2. I think this recipe is perfect using my Chick Fil A Sauce (a Honey BBQ based sauce). It makes it so much like a Chick Fil A Wrap. That being said there are lots of other sauces that would be great in this. BBQ Sauce Ranch Dressing Garlic Aioli Pesto Sriracha Mayonnaise
  3. I think one of the best parts about this recipe is the crispy outside and melty cheese inside that you get from grilling them. To grill them: Fold the outsides of the wrap in on itself. Then roll it up. Place it seam side down on the hot grill. Flip it over after 2 to 3 minutes, and grill it for 2 to 3 more minutes. If it starts to open up on the grill after flipping, stick a tooth pick through it to secure it.
